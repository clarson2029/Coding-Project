    Respond & Make a Difference

    Students across campus are facing real challenges as they try to balance academic pressure, wellbeing, and daily life — and meaningful change starts with people who are willing to respond. Whether you’ve seen these struggles firsthand or simply want to support those around you, your actions matter. This page offers simple ways you can get involved, make students feel seen, and help build a healthier, more supportive community for everyone.

    • Teachers
    • Parents

    Supporting Yourself Through the Challenge

    Balancing academics and wellbeing is difficult, and it’s okay to acknowledge that. Small, intentional habits can make a meaningful difference in managing stress and staying grounded.

    • Reach out to friends, mentors, or counselors when you feel overwhelmed.
    • Create small, manageable goals instead of trying to tackle everything at once.
    • Take breaks without feeling guilty — rest is part of productivity.
    • Stay connected to activities and people who bring you joy.
    • Remember that asking for help is a sign of strength, not weakness.
