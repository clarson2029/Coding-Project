    What Shapes Student Success? Exploring the Factors Behind Academic Performance

    This project examines the key influences on student academic performance by analyzing how family background, student behavior, and classroom environment interact to shape outcomes. Through a series of data visualizations, we explore the roles of parental support, individual effort, access to resources, and teacher quality in affecting exam scores and attendance. Together, these pages provide a data-driven look at the factors that most strongly contribute to student success beyond test scores alone.

    This section examines how students’ own behaviors—such as studying, attendance, and tutoring—directly influence academic outcomes and exam performance.

    Interactive Page: How to Factors Combine to Affect the Student Body

    This interactive page allows users to explore how multiple aspects of student life work together to influence academic performance. By examining exam scores alongside hours studied, physical activity, and sleep duration, the visualization highlights how academic effort and personal well-being intersect. Rather than isolating a single factor, this page emphasizes that student outcomes are shaped by a combination of behaviors, illustrating the cumulative impact of study habits, health, and balance on overall performance.
